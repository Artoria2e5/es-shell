I've played with it quite a bit.  The code is really cruddy; even the
mit version has a bunch of bugs.  But despite that, the thing is really
cool.  After a few days of use it knows things about you that you don't
know yourself. :-) Really, it's uncanny when you edit a file, and it
prompts you with the next three commands you were just about to type.
Next to first class continuations, markov chains are the neatest
thing.
