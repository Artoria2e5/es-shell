Try doing "stty -echo" as the first thing in es.  If you linked es with
readline, that should stop the repetition.

If you linked es with editline, you're screwed, because editline insists on
thinking it knows better than you do about whether you want echo on or off,
and will do you the "favor" of turning it back on no matter what.  Probably
this bug ought to be fixed.
