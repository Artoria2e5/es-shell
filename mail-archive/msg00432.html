In other words, make `let' truly be let, and not let*?  Yes, I think this
is a good idea.  You can get the same effect as let* by doing some of the
assignments inside the body of the let, or by nested lets.  Getting the
flavor of `let' with let*'s behavior is more difficult.  :-)
